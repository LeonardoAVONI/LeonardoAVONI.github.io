Supersonic Wind Tunnel
Design & Testing

Design, manufacturing and experimental testing of a small-scale supersonic wind tunnel.

Overview

Our team designed and built a small-scale supersonic wind tunnel reaching Mach 1.5–2.5. Full cycle: aero design → structural sizing → manufacturing → instrumentation → experimental validation.

Nozzle Geometry

Method of Characteristics (MoC) nozzle contour for uniform supersonic flow. Area ratios A*/A computed per Mach number; wall profiles generated numerically.

Structural & Sealing Design

Tunnel body sized for pressure differentials. Acrylic optical windows sealed for schlieren. Parts made by CNC machining and 3D printing.

Pitot-Static Measurements

Mach number verified via Rayleigh Pitot formula — good agreement with design targets.

Schlieren Visualisation

Z-type schlieren setup revealing shock waves, expansion fans, and boundary layers.

Shock Wave Analysis

Oblique shock angles measured from schlieren images vs. theoretical predictions — strong agreement.
