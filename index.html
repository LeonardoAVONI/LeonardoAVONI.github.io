Where I Come From

Born in Bologna, Italy, I developed a focused interest in mechanical engineering at a very young age. This led me to pursue my education in France, where I graduated with a Scientific Baccalauréat (19.27/20) specializing in Engineering Sciences. My trajectory has been defined by a commitment to technical precision and international mobility.

I completed my Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees at EPFL (École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne). The curriculum provided a rigorous mathematical and physical foundation through advanced Analysis, Algebra, and Physics. As I progressed, I acquired a broad-spectrum mechanical engineering toolkit, covering:

  • Core Mechanics: Structural mechanics, metals and alloys, and production processes.
  • Systems & Control: Electronics, electric motors, and informatics.
  • Fluid Dynamics: Mechanical design and fluid mechanics, later specializing in Aerodynamics and Space Technologies.

This "generalist" approach at EPFL ensured I wasn't confined to a single niche, but rather capable of navigating any mechanical system from first principles. I concluded these five years with a Master’s project at the LIS Lab, focusing on the automated wind tunnel characterization of a morphing drone—a project requiring a synthesis of fluid dynamics, hardware integration, and coding.

What I Do

Professionally: The Theory-to-Application Bridge

My work centers on converting complex aerospace theory into functional tools and hardware. Since November 2023, I have been conducting my PhD at ISAE-Supaero, focusing on the dynamics of highly flexible aircraft (e.g., Solar Impulse, Helios).

Key technical contributions include:

  • Software Development: Created WingLoop, an automation and control library presented at the AIAA AVIATION Forum 2025.
  • System Documentation: Authored the Extended User Manual for ASWING, featured on the official MIT/Drela website.
  • Industrial Experience: Two internships at Synova SA focused on machine calibration and stability protocols.
  • Prototyping: Designed and built a quadcopter from the ground up to master the full stack of UAV hardware and firmware design.
