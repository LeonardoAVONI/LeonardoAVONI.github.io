3D Printed Projects
Design & Fabrication

A growing collection of functional and creative designs — from drone parts to artistic pieces.

Overview

3D printing is a core tool in my workflow — for prototypes, final-use parts, jigs, and creative work.

Drone Frame Components

Motor mounts, landing gear, gimbal frames, antenna holders for the quadcopter build. Design criteria: vibration resistance, minimum weight, impact toughness. Printed in PETG.

Wind Tunnel Fixtures

Mounting brackets and probe holders for the EPFL wind tunnel project — accurate to ±0.2 mm and aerodynamically unobtrusive.

Lab Tools & Jigs

Cable clips, sensor holders, alignment jigs, custom electronics enclosures.

Creative Prints

Geometric sculptures, personalised gifts, guitar accessories. Resin printing for high-detail pieces.
