Synova SA Internship
Laser Jet Calibration

Precision calibration of laser microjet cutting systems at Synova SA — where optics meets fluid mechanics.

About Synova

Synova SA pioneers Laser MicroJet (LMJ) technology — guiding a laser inside a hair-thin water jet. Applications: aerospace turbine blades, semiconductor wafers, medical devices.

Optical Alignment

Calibration protocol for beam-steering mirrors, focusing optics, and the coupling chamber. Beam quality (M², waist, divergence) measured with a beam profiler camera.

Fluidic Sub-System

Nozzle pressure–flow-rate calibration. Orifice geometry (50–150 µm) measured by optical microscopy to detect wear and enforce replacement thresholds.

Metrology & Documentation

All procedures documented in a service-team calibration manual. SPC charts established for machine-to-machine variability monitoring.
